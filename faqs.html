F.A.Q.s



1. Why do you need window screens?

Protection:
Window screens keep insects, animals, leaves, and debris from getting inside the home. They also offer protection against the elements. An open window with a screen will allow less water in during a downpour than one without a screen.

Fresh airflow:
Screens permit fresh airflow, while repelling insects and debris.

5. What are the different window screen wires?

Standard fiberglass:
is a flexible screen that does not crease, dent, or unravel. The most commonly used window and door screening for new construction and replacement window screens. Charcoal color is the most popular as it has the best outward visibility.

Aluminum Wire:
is a strong and durable insect screening that resists rust and will not sag. A protective finish prevents corrosion and strengthens the weave. Charcoal and Black aluminum screen finish reduce glare for greater outward visibility..


6. Do you also install patio screens?

Yes, we also make patio screens. For pet owners, we recommend thicker heavy duty fiberglass screens to limit the chance of your pets leaving.
